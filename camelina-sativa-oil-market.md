<p><p>The Camelina sativa oil market includes two primary extraction methods: Hexane Extraction and Cold Pressing Extraction. Hexane-extracted Oil (HEO) is derived using hexane solvents, leading to higher yields and longer shelf life, making it suitable for industrial applications. In contrast, Cold-pressed Oil (CPO) is extracted mechanically without heat, preserving the oil’s natural nutrients and flavor, appealing to health-conscious consumers. Both methods cater to different market segments, with HEO typically favored for commercial use and CPO for premium, organic products.</p></p>
