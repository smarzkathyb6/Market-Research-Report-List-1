<p><p>The landing gear market comprises various types suited for different aircraft and operational needs. Strut landing gear features upright supports for stability and is common in smaller aircraft. Rocker landing gear utilizes a pivoting mechanism for enhanced shock absorption, ideal for off-road applications. Framed landing gear incorporates a rigid frame structure, providing strength and support for larger aircraft. Pontoon landing gear is designed for water landings, allowing amphibious capabilities. Each type serves unique functionality and performance requirements in aviation.</p></p>
