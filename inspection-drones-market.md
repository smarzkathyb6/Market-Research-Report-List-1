<p><p>The inspection drone market is primarily divided into two types: fixed-wing and rotor-wing drones. Fixed-wing drones feature a long, aerodynamic design allowing for extended flight times and covering large areas, making them ideal for infrastructure inspections like pipelines or power lines. Rotor-wing drones, typically multi-rotor, offer vertical takeoff and landing capabilities, enabling precise maneuverability and the ability to inspect intricate structures such as buildings and wind turbines. Each type serves distinct applications based on operational needs and site conditions.</p></p>
