<p><p>Product Information Management (PIM) solutions can be categorized into two main types: multi-domain and single-domain markets. Multi-domain PIM systems manage diverse product data across various categories, such as physical goods, digital products, or services, catering to complex, large-scale enterprises with broad product portfolios. In contrast, single-domain PIM focuses on a specific product type or category, providing streamlined data management and optimization for businesses with a narrower product range. Both types aim to enhance product data accuracy and improve operational efficiency.</p></p>
