<p><p>The low-intensity natural sweetener market is primarily divided into two categories: nutritive and non-nutritive sweeteners. Nutritive sweeteners, such as honey and agave syrup, provide calories and are derived from natural sources. They can influence blood sugar levels. In contrast, non-nutritive sweeteners, like stevia or monk fruit extract, offer sweetness without significant calories and have minimal impact on blood sugar. Both types cater to health-conscious consumers, supporting sugar reduction while enhancing flavor in food and beverages.</p></p>
