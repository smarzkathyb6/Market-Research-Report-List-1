<p><ul><li>Trivalent OPV</li><li>Bivalent OPV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oral Polio Vaccines (OPV) are crucial for preventing poliovirus transmission. The market consists primarily of two types: Trivalent OPV (tOPV) and Bivalent OPV (bOPV). Trivalent OPV provides immunity against all three poliovirus strains, while Bivalent OPV focuses on types 1 and 3, omitting type 2 to mitigate the risk of vaccine-derived poliovirus. The choice between these vaccines affects public health strategies and vaccination campaigns, particularly in regions where polio remains a threat. Each type plays a vital role in global eradication efforts.</p></p>
