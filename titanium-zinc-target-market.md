<p><p>The Titanium Zinc Target market is segmented into two primary types: Plane Targets and Rotating Targets. Plane Targets are flat surfaces used in various deposition processes, providing a uniform coating on substrates. They are widely utilized in applications like electronics and solar cells. Rotating Targets, on the other hand, are cylindrical and designed to enhance the deposition efficiency by facilitating continuous rotation during the sputtering process. This increases the target's lifespan and improves material utilization, making them ideal for high-volume production environments.</p></p>
