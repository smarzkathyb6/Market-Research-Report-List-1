<p><p>The sorghum seed market includes three main types: grain sorghum, forage sorghum, and sweet sorghum. Grain sorghum is primarily cultivated for human and animal food, known for its drought resistance and high yield. Forage sorghum is aimed at livestock feed, providing nutritious pastures or silage. Sweet sorghum, characterized by its high sugar content, is mainly used for syrup production and biofuel. Each type serves distinct markets, driven by agricultural, nutritional, and energy needs.</p></p>
