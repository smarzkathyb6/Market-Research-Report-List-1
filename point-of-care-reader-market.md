<p><p>The Point of Care Reader market includes two main types: mobile readers and benchtop readers. Mobile readers are compact, portable devices designed for on-site testing and immediate results, making them ideal for remote locations and emergency situations. In contrast, benchtop readers are larger, typically used in labs or clinics, offering extensive testing capabilities and higher throughput. Both types enhance patient care by enabling timely diagnostics, but they cater to different settings and needs within the healthcare ecosystem.</p></p>
