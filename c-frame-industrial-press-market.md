<p><p>C-frame industrial presses are categorized into three market types based on automation levels: fully-automatic, semi-automatic, and manual presses. Fully-automatic presses require minimal human intervention, operating continuously for high-volume production, enhancing efficiency and precision. Semi-automatic presses combine automated processes with some manual actions, offering flexibility and moderate output. Manual presses depend entirely on operator skills, suitable for low-volume tasks or specialized applications. Each type serves distinct industry needs, balancing productivity and operator involvement.</p></p>
