<p><ul><li>Multimode</li><li>Single-mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multi-wavelength laser module market is divided into two primary types: multimode and single-mode. Multimode lasers transmit multiple light modes simultaneously, enabling higher power and broader bandwidth, making them ideal for short-distance applications like local area networks. In contrast, single-mode lasers focus on a single light path, ensuring higher precision and longer distances, making them suitable for telecommunications and data transmission over longer spans. Each type serves distinct applications based on distance, bandwidth requirements, and communication systems.</p></p>
