<p><ul><li>Suction Ultrasonic Scalpel</li><li>Cutting Ultrasonic Scalpel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasound scalpel market is primarily categorized into two types: suction ultrasonic scalpels and cutting ultrasonic scalpels. Suction ultrasonic scalpels combine ultrasonic energy with suction capabilities, effectively cutting tissue while simultaneously removing blood and fluid, enhancing visibility during procedures. In contrast, cutting ultrasonic scalpels focus on precise tissue dissection using high-frequency vibrations, enabling controlled cutting with minimal thermal damage. Both types offer advantages in surgical precision, reduced bleeding, and improved recovery times, catering to various surgical specialties.</p></p>
