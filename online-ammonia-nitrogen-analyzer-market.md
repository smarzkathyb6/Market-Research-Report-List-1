<p><p>The Online Ammonia Nitrogen Analyzer market comprises three main types: Ion-selective Electrodes (ISE), Ultraviolet (UV) Absorbance, and Colorimetric methods. ISE technology measures ammonia concentration based on ion-selectivity, providing rapid and accurate results. UV Absorbance utilizes light absorption properties of ammonia, allowing for continuous monitoring in aqueous samples. Colorimetric methods involve chemical reactions that produce a color change proportional to ammonia concentration, enabling quantification. Each technology offers distinct advantages for real-time ammonia analysis in various industrial applications.</p></p>
