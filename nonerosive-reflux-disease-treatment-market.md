<p><p>The Nonerosive Reflux Disease (NERD) treatment market comprises several types of medications. Proton pump inhibitors (PPIs) are the most effective, reducing stomach acid production and alleviating symptoms. H2 receptor antagonists also lower acid secretion but are generally less potent than PPIs. Antacids provide quick relief by neutralizing existing stomach acid. Other treatments may include herbal remedies and lifestyle modifications. These various options cater to differing patient needs, preferences, and severity of symptoms in managing NERD.</p></p>
