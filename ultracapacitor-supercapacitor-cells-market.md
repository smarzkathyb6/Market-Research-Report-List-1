<p><p>Ultracapacitors, also known as supercapacitors, are energy storage devices that store electrical energy through electrostatic charge separation. They bridge the gap between traditional capacitors and batteries, offering high power density, rapid charge and discharge capabilities, and extensive cycling stability. These features make ultracapacitors ideal for applications requiring quick bursts of energy, such as in electric vehicles, renewable energy systems, and various consumer electronics.</p><p>The Ultracapacitor (Supercapacitor) Cells Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.5% during the forecast period.
