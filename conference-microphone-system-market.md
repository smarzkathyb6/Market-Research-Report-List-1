<p><p>The conference microphone system market is divided into wired and wireless types. Wired systems offer reliable audio transmission with minimal interference, making them ideal for fixed setups. They typically require cables and connectors, which can limit mobility but ensure consistent performance. In contrast, wireless microphone systems provide greater flexibility and mobility, allowing speakers to move freely without being tethered. These systems use radio frequencies to transmit audio, making them suitable for dynamic environments but requiring careful management of battery life and possible interference.</p></p>
