<p><p>The hepatitis testing diagnosis market comprises various types of tests, each serving unique purposes. Enzyme-linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) is widely used for detecting antibodies and antigens. Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDT) offer quick results, often requiring minimal training. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) is a highly sensitive method for detecting viral RNA. Isothermal Nucleic Acid Amplification Technology (INAAT) allows amplification of nucleic acids at a constant temperature. Other methods may include traditional culture tests and serological assays, expanding diagnostic options.</p></p>
