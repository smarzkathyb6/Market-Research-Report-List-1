<p><p>The medical heating blanket market is segmented into three types: full body, lower body, and upper body. Full body heating blankets provide comprehensive warmth and are often used for patients requiring overall thermal therapy. Lower body blankets focus on areas such as the legs and feet, catering to individuals with localized pain or circulation issues. Upper body blankets are designed to warm the torso and arms, ideal for patients recovering from surgery or experiencing upper body ailments, ensuring targeted thermal relief.</p></p>
