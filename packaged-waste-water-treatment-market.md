<p><p>The packaged wastewater treatment market comprises several key technologies. Membrane Bioreactor (MBR) combines biological treatment with membrane filtration, offering high-quality effluent. Moving Bed Biofilm Reactor (MBBR) uses suspended carrier media for biofilm growth, enhancing treatment efficiency in smaller footprints. Sequencing Batch Reactor (SBR) operates in batches, allowing flexibility and effective nutrient removal. Extended aeration involves prolonged aeration for better organic matter degradation. Each type caters to various scales and treatment needs, making them essential for efficient wastewater management.</p></p>
