<p><p>The radio masts and towers market consists of various types designed for communication needs. Lattice towers are typically truss-like structures offering high strength and flexibility. Guyed towers utilize cables for support, providing stability and height with less material. Monopole towers are single cylindrical structures, ideal for urban environments due to their small footprint. Stealth towers are designed to blend into surroundings, resembling elements like trees or flagpoles. Other types encompass a range of specialized or hybrid designs catering to specific applications.</p></p>
