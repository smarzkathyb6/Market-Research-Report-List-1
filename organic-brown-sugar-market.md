<p><p>The organic brown sugar market comprises three main types: light brown sugar, dark brown sugar, and regular brown sugar. Light brown sugar contains less molasses and has a mild flavor, making it ideal for baking and sweetening beverages. Dark brown sugar has a higher molasses content, providing a richer taste and color, often preferred in recipes requiring deeper sweetness. Regular brown sugar typically refers to a standard blend of white and molasses, appealing to general consumers looking for versatile sweetening options.</p></p>
