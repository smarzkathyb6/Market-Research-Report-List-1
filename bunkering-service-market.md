<p><p>The bunkering service market encompasses various fuel types for maritime operations. Marine Diesel Oil (MDO) is a refined fuel suited for marine engines, while Marine Gasoil (MGO) offers a lower sulfur alternative, complying with emission regulations. Marine Fuel Oil (MFO) and Intermediate Fuel Oil (IFO) are heavier oils used primarily for larger vessels, with IFO blending lighter and heavier oils. Heavy Fuel Oil (HFO), the thickest among these, is cost-effective but requires additional treatment for efficient combustion in ships.</p></p>
