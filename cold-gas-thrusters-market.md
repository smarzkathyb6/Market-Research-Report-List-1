<p><p>Cold gas thrusters are propulsion systems that utilize compressed gas stored in a tank to generate thrust for maneuvering spacecraft or satellites. They operate by releasing the gas through a nozzle, which results in a reactive force that allows for precise movements in space or controlled descent. Due to their simplicity, reliability, and ease of integration, cold gas thrusters are increasingly favored for applications in small satellites, deep-space exploration missions, and orbital maneuvering.</p><p>The Cold Gas Thrusters Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.1% during the forecast period. Emerging technologies such as hybrid propulsion systems and improvements in gas storage solutions are also shaping the market.
