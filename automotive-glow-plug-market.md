<p><p>The automotive glow plug market is primarily divided into two types: metal glow plugs and ceramic glow plugs. Metal glow plugs are made of steel and have a quicker heating time, making them effective for cold starts in diesel engines. Conversely, ceramic glow plugs, made from advanced materials, offer higher thermal efficiency and longevity, enabling better performance and reduced emissions. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing engine reliability and efficiency, catering to the needs of varied vehicle applications.</p></p>
