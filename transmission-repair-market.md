<p><strong>The Transmission Repair Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transmission General Repair</li><li>Transmission Overhaul</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transmission repair market comprises two main types: general repair and overhaul. General repair focuses on addressing specific issues, such as fluid leaks or sensor replacements, to restore functionality without extensive disassembly. In contrast, transmission overhaul involves a comprehensive disassembly of the transmission system to replace worn components and rebuild it to factory specifications, ensuring optimal performance. Both services cater to different customer needs, with general repairs requiring less time and cost, while overhauls offer long-term reliability.</p></p>
