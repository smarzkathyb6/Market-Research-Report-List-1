<p><strong>The Semiconductor Third-party Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laboratory Testing</li><li>Wafer Testing and Final Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor third-party testing market comprises three key segments: laboratory testing, wafer testing, and final testing. Laboratory testing involves evaluating the materials and processes used in chip fabrication for quality assurance. Wafer testing assesses the electrical performance of semiconductor wafers before slicing into individual chips, ensuring functionality. Final testing is conducted on packaged chips to verify overall operational performance and reliability in real-world applications. Together, these testing types ensure the integrity and performance of semiconductor devices throughout their production lifecycle.</p></p>
