<p><p>The aluminum curtain wall market comprises two primary types: semi-unitized and unitized systems. Semi-unitized systems involve prefabricated panels that are partially assembled in the factory and then completed on-site, offering a balance of efficiency and flexibility. In contrast, unitized systems are fully assembled in the factory and delivered as complete units, allowing for quicker installation and reduced labor costs on-site. Both types provide aesthetic and structural benefits, catering to varying architectural needs and construction timelines.</p></p>
