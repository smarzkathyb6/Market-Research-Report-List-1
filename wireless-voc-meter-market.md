<p><strong>The Wireless VOC Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless VOC meter market primarily includes two types: PID (Photoionization Detectors) and Metal-Oxide Semiconductors. PID sensors utilize ultraviolet light to ionize volatile organic compounds, providing rapid and accurate measurements, making them ideal for detecting low concentrations in various environments. In contrast, Metal-Oxide Semiconductor sensors function based on the resistance change of metal oxides when exposed to VOCs, offering cost-effectiveness and durability, albeit with potentially lower sensitivity. Both types serve crucial roles in environmental monitoring and indoor air quality assessments.</p></p>
